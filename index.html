ABOUT ME

I am a 19-year-old Igorot from the beautiful town of Sagada, a place that has shaped my character, values, and understanding of hard work. Growing up in Sagada taught me the importance of professionalism and dedication because many of my early experiences involved working closely with people in hotel and restaurant settings. Through these roles, I learned how essential communication, teamwork, and proper management are in creating a smooth and welcoming environment. These experiences did more than teach me skills—they instilled in me discipline, patience, and respect for every kind of work.

Living and working in Sagada helped me develop a strong work ethic that continues to guide me today. It made me curious and eager to learn new things, especially as I move toward my dream of becoming an IT professional. I am ready to face new challenges, gain hands-on experience, and constantly improve myself. With a foundation built on real-life responsibilities and the desire to keep growing, I aim to build a meaningful career in the field of Information Technology while carrying the values of my Igorot heritage wherever I go.
